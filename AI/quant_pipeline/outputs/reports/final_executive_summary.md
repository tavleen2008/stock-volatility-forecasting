# Final Executive Summary

## Project Goal
Forecasting short-term realized volatility using advanced linear models (HAR, HAR Log, HAR+Sentiment) and tree-based machine learning (XGBoost, XGBoost+Sentiment) architectures for high-market-cap equities.

## Key Findings
- HAR with log target transformation improves performance by stabilizing the heavy-tailed residuals.
- Realized quarticity and absolute returns remain the most statistically significant drivers of tomorrow's volatility.
